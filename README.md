### CarsLine (Template)
Carsline is an online auto vehicle portal which is used for real world portal with Dynamic range and Responsive Layout.

![Index Page](https://github.com/nandpalmohit/CarsLine/blob/main/ss/index.PNG)

## Category of Cars
- Top Selling Cars
- New Cars
- Upcoming Cars
- Top Rating Cars
- Top Safety Cars
- Car Brands
- Comparision of Cars
